Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your data stays on your device

Ledger stores all your spending data — entries, goals, and settings — locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal spending data on external servers.

What we store locally

  • Spending entries (amount, category, note, timestamp)
  • Savings goals and progress
  • App settings (hourly rate, reminder preferences)

No account required

Ledger works entirely offline. You do not need to create an account, and we never ask for your name, email, or any identifying information to use the app.

In-app purchases

If you choose to upgrade to Ledger Premium, your purchase is processed by the Apple App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify subscriptions — RevenueCat receives purchase transaction data (via an anonymous identifier, not linked to your name or identity) solely to confirm your subscription status and power basic account functionality and analytics on our end. We do not store your payment details, and RevenueCat does not have access to your spending data.

No data sharing or advertising

We do not share, sell, or transmit your spending data to any third parties. Ledger contains no advertising networks and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future version, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact
